Mr. Teleporter

"Hey Google, let me talk to Mr. Teleporter."

Getting Started

  1. Go to https://mr-teleport.appspot.com/ in your VR headset, and select/click Full Screen Toggle for the best visual experience
  2. Say Hey Google, let me talk to Mr. Teleporter in front of a device with Google Assistant installed
  3. After being prompted, try teleporting to a country like France by saying teleport me to France
  4. After being prompted, try teleporting to a city like Beijing by saying teleport me to the city of Beijing
  5. That's it! You have the ability to visit places around the world

Commands

When prompted use any of the commands below

teleport me to <country>: Teleport to the country of your choice

teleport me to the city of <city>: Teleport to the city of your choice

teleport away: Leave Mr. Teleporter
